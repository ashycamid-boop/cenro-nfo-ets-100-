Participant Assignment Workspace

Assign eligible applicants into the yearly batch and place PDOs into Groups 1, 2, or 3 for training.

Assignment Rules

  • Only training-eligible applicants can be assigned.
  • The yearly batch limit follows the live program capacity.
  • Exactly 3 groups are used.
  • Each group limit follows the current live batch size.
  • Participants stay under their assigned PDO.
  • Multiple PDOs may share the same training group.
