Eligibility Rules

  • Eligibility is based only on the latest application.
  • Assigned PDO is required.
  • All required initial uploads must exist.
  • All required uploads must be reviewed by the assigned PDO.
  • All required uploads must be verified/approved.
  • Final approval is not required for training eligibility.
